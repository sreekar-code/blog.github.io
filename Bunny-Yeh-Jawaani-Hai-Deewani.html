Bunny—Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

26 Feb, 2024

Main udna chahta hoon,
daudna chahta hoon,
girna bhi chahta hoon...
bus rukna nahi chahta

Those words? They completely swept the 17-year-old me off my feet for Bunny!

Back then, Bunny was everything I thought was cool. Traveling world, charming girls, and following heart—he had it all!

Cut to adulthood, Bunny became less cool for me. Why? 'Cause he was always on the move, had no lasting relationships, and barely made any time for friends.

Bunny was lucky though—he had Aditi, Avi, and Naina. They stuck by him, even though he wasn't the best at nurturing friendships.

But the truth is, friendships and relationships require continuous effort. Neglect them, and the dynamics shift, eventually leading to losing your friends.

So, the 25-year-old me now, would totally fall for a Bunny who'd say:

Main udna chahta hoon,
daudna chahta hoon,
girna bhi chahta hoon...
apnon ke saath rehna bhi chahta hoon
chahe iske liye thodi der rukna bhi pade.

And lastly, Bunny, tu right nahi hai, bas reality se bahut alag hai!
