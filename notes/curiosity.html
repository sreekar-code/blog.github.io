Curiosity

🌿 budding  ·  last watered 23 Jun, 2026

I have a child-like curiosity and I never want to lose it.

I've been into so many things. Poetry. Short stories. Freelance writing. Marketing. Chess. Running. Cycling. Sci-fi novels. Coffee brewing. Physics. Travel. I go deep into something, get completely obsessed, and then move on to the next thing.

People see this as inconsistency. I see it differently.

Every thing I've been curious about left something behind. A lens. A way of looking at problems. When I'm stuck on something now, I notice all these past versions of me chipping in.

The more dots you collect, the more connections you can make.

I don't know where my curiosity will take me next. But I've stopped worrying about that. The collecting is the point.
