Writing as thinking

🌱 seed  ·  last watered 23 Jun, 2026

Most of my blog posts start as a feeling I can't name yet.

I sit down to write and I don't really know what I think. But somewhere in the middle of writing, it becomes clear. Like the act of writing is what does the thinking, not the other way around.

I haven't fully figured out why this works. But I notice that when I don't write, ideas just float around and stay fuzzy. The moment I start putting them into sentences, I find out quickly what I actually believe and what I was just assuming.

Maybe writing is less about expression and more about discovery.

Still not sure. Still writing to find out.
