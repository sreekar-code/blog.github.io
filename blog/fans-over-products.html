Fans over products

4th March, 2026

One indie hacker made $15k in less than a week!

When I first heard this, I was mind-blown. That's crazy ROI for three days of work.

After the initial awe, I spent some time thinking about how he pulled it off. Was it the idea? I don't think so. Plenty of people probably had the same idea. Was it the execution? Not really. Anyone can execute ideas now with all the powerful tools available.

So what's the secret sauce?

His brand.

A brand isn't an app you can build in a few minutes. It takes time. It takes effort. And it demands a ton of patience. This guy has built his audience and earned his 1000 true fans. That's what made $15k in three days possible.

Someone without that context might think his success is easy to emulate. So they build an app. It doesn't hit, so they build another. Then another. They end up stuck in a loop of shipping things while their brand stays at level zero. That's a recipe for failure.

The truth is anyone can build stuff now. But a product without an audience behind it is just noise on the internet.

Build things. Build them for fun. But if you want to make real money, invest energy into your brand too. Build the audience. Earn your 1000 true fans!

The product is temporary. The brand is the asset.
