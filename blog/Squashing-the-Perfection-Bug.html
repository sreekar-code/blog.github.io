Squashing the Perfection Bug

31 May, 2024

I love my blog! I really do! I want to write every day. But I don't. Why? Because of the Perfection Bug!

"You need to share a unique thought"

"Your idea should be worth penning"

"You need to write in great detail"

Buzz, buzzz, buzzzz....the perfection bug keeps buzzing in my ear.

But today, I decided to squash that bug!

I don't need unique thoughts. I don't need amazing ideas. I don't need to explain things in detail.

All I need is a thought (which can be ordinary) and a bunch of words to share it. That's it! That's all I need to write on my blog.

So, see you again tomorrow with an ordinary thought and a bunch of words!
