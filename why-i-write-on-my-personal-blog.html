Why I Write on My Personal Blog

27 June, 2024

I'm a freelance content writer. I write for companies. I write on my LinkedIn. And I write on my Twitter. So, I write more, and write often!

Yet, I still write on my personal blog.

Why?

Because unlike companies and social media, my blog lets me write whatever I want to write with no strings attached.

On my blog, I dump ideas, share my thoughts, and express what I truly feel.

Plus, I don't convince anyone into anything, please anyone, seek validation from anyone, or worry to be politically correct.

I write! I just write!

And this freedom to write whatever the fuck I want to write is why I write on my personal blog.
