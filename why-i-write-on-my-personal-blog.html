Why I Write on My Personal Blog

27 June, 2024

I'm a freelance content writer. I write for companies. I write on my LinkedIn. And I write on my Twitter. So, I write more, and write often!

Yet, I still write on my personal blog.

Why?

Because unlike companies and social media, my blog lets me write what I really want to write. Plus, I get to decide who reads what I write.

On my blog, I write about movies, podcasts, YouTube videos, books, and blogs. Plus, I share my happy moments, sad moments, things I like, things I don't like, things I'm excited about, things I'm scared, and things I'm grateful for.

All, while not convincing anyone into anything, pleasing anyone, seeking validation from anyone, or worrying to be politically correct.

And I don't promote my blog. I only share it with my close friends. They read it and share their views. That's enough for me. Actually, more than enough!

So, this control over what I write plus who can read what I write is why I write on my personal blog.
